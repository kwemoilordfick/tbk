Sustainable Beekeeping Practices:
  • Posted Feb 12, 2024
  • by Francis Pulisi

Tirinyi Beekeepers prioritize sustainable beekeeping practices to
ensure the health and longevity of their colonies. They refrain
from using harmful chemicals and pesticides, opting instead for natural
methods that promote the bees' well-being. By adopting these environmentally
friendly practices, Tirinyi Beekeepers contribute to the overall
health of the local ecosystem.

Beekeeping Training Programs
  • Posted Feb 12, 2024
  • by Changa David

Tirinyi Beekeepers host regular training programs to share their expertise
with aspiring beekeepers in the community. These initiatives not only promote
the growth of the beekeeping industry but also provide community members with
valuable skills that can improve their livelihoods.
